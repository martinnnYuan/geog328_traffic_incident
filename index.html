Mapping Seattle Traffic Risk in 2022

We combine SDOT 2022 collision data with 2022 traffic flow counts to highlight locations where crashes are high relative to traffic volume. Use the interactive map to explore crashes, volumes, and our risk index, then review the team and methods behind the work.

Open Map Meet the Team

2022 Focus

All datasets are from 2022: SDOT collision records and traffic flow counts. Legends and labels keep the year front and center.

Risk Index

We snap collisions to the nearest traffic segment and compute crashes per volume to reveal segments that punch above their weight in risk.

Layered View

Mapbox-based base map, thematic overlays for collisions and flow, and labels kept on top for clarity. Legends explain the symbology.

Actionable Insights

Identify spots with low traffic but high crashes that may indicate design, maintenance, or signage issues worth prioritizing.
